Captured Moments Photography website featuring services showcased in the hero section.
To follow the non-disclosure agreement, I have removed and hidden confidential details and the project name in this case study. All information here is my own and may not reflect the views of the Client or ArrayPointer. 
Role UX Designer, UX Researcher, UI Designer
Team Solo
Duration 1 week
Problem
Lack of clarity regarding available services, work samples, and clear messaging for users
Objective
To create a visually engaging hero section that effectively communicates the core services of Captured Moments, enhancing user engagement and facilitating a smooth user journey from entry point to service inquiry. 
Design Approach
The hero section is designed to immediately grab the visitor’s attention with a compelling visual narrative that aligns with the brand’s identity. This section combines a striking image, clear typography, and strategic call-to-action elements.  
Problem Solving
Attention and Retention
Challenge
The initial challenge was ensuring that visitors’ attention was captured and retained long enough to convey the key messages.
Solution
The visually appealing image grid immediately draws visitors in, while the bold and engaging typography holds their attention.
Service Communication
Challenge
Visitors struggled to quickly understand the range of services offered by Captured Moments. This lack of clarity could lead to confusion and a higher bounce rate, as potential clients might leave the site without finding the specific service they need.
Solution
This layout allows visitors to immediately see and navigate to the type of photography they are interested in. The clear labels and organized presentation reduce confusion and help users find relevant information quickly.
User Engagement
Challenge
Encouraging visitors to take the next step, whether contacting Captured Moments or requesting a quotation.
Solution
Clear and compelling calls-to-action are strategically placed to guide users towards interaction. The “Contact” and “Get Quotation” buttons are prominently displayed and easy to access, reducing friction in the user journey.
Outcome
The redesigned hero section successfully addresses the initial challenges by combining visual appeal, clear communication, and strategic calls-to-action. This has resulted in:
Increased Engagement
The captivating design and personal messaging have led to longer visitor retention times. 
Higher Conversion Rates
The clear calls-to-action and organized service presentation have improved the rate of inquiries and service requests. 
Enhanced User Experience
Visitors can quickly understand the services offered and feel a personal connection with Captured Moments, leading to a more satisfying and effective user experience. 
This case study highlights the thoughtful design process behind the hero section of Captured Moments website. By solving key challenges related to attention, communication, and engagement, the design not only enhances the visual appeal but also plays a crucial role in generating leads and growing the business.
