Credibility Redesigning the Credibility website to showcase services and enhance user engagement.
To follow the non-disclosure agreement, I have removed and hidden confidential details and the project name in this case study. All information here is my own and may not reflect the views of the Client or ArrayPointer. 
Role UX Designer, UX Researcher, UI Designer
Team Solo
Duration 2 week
Problem
Credibility original hero section lacked interactivity and clear messaging, leading to low user engagement and limited understanding of their services.
Objective
To create an engaging and informative hero section that effectively communicates Credibility value proposition and services, boosts user engagement, and encourages potential clients to make contact. 
Design Approach
centered on creating an interactive and engaging hero section that highlights Credibility key services through a dynamic background slider, clear messaging, and a prominent CTA.
Problem Solving 
Interactive Background Slider
Implemented an automatic background slider that changes images to highlight different aspects of Credibility's offerings, enhancing the visual experience for each service.
Services included in the slider
Operational Mastery, Employee Growtht, Technology Leadership, Strategic Planning
As the slider automatically transitions, the background image updates to match the featured service, providing visual context and boosting user engagement.
Clear Value Proposition and Service Highlighting
Introduced a concise and impactful headline Streamlining Success.
Added a brief yet informative subheading: "Streamlining Success enhances efficiency by refining processes and reducing waste, helping organizations reach their full potential." 
Each service in the slider is accompanied by a small description. When a service is highlighted, it shows a more detailed description and a related image, making it easier for users to understand the offerings. 
Prominent Call-to-Action
Designed a clear and prominent CTA button labeled "Contact now" to encourage immediate action from visitors.
Positioned the CTA strategically within the hero section to ensure visibility and accessibility. 
Implementation and Results 
User Engagement
Post-implementation, there was a noticeable increase in user engagement metrics, including longer time spent on the homepage and reduced bounce rates.  
Clarity in Messaging
The clear and concise messaging helped users quickly understand Credibility value proposition and services, leading to better user comprehension and interest. 
Service Highlighting
The interactive background slider allowed users to explore Credibility key services in an engaging manner, making it easier for them to understand and relate to each service offering. 
Conversion Rates
The redesigned hero section led to a significant increase in the number of users clicking the CTA and making contact, thus generating more leads for Credibility. 
The redesigned hero section for Credibility effectively solved initial challenges by creating a visually appealing, informative, and engaging entry point. The interactive background slider, highlighting the four key services, captured user interest and clarified Credibility offerings. This project enhanced the user experience and significantly boosted lead generation and client engagement.
